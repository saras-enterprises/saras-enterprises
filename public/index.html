About Us

Saras Enterprises is a Pune, India based company, established in 1993 with a sole purpose of providing the best CNC machined components in the market. Over 3 decades, we have been fulfilling highly critical/ complex machining requirements with highest quality and successfully meeting production targets.

Our belief in developing long lasting relationships with our customers was further encouraged when Bharat Electronics Ltd., Pashan, Pune honored us as “THE SUPPLIER OF THE YEAR” and “ZERO DEFECT SUPPLIER” as well.

We will be grateful if you give us an opportunity for development and production of components needed by you. Our commitment to quality work at competitive prices supported by in time delivery will continue to satisfy our valued customers.

Arvind Habade

CEO

The founder aka the soul of SARAS Enterprises, established the company with a motive to serve the best CNC machined products in the market. Starting from scratch that means learning the basics of machining principles, he has mastered the art of churning precision components from possibly all types of materials along with a high productivity. So far in the last 3 decades, he has never stopped delivering the best products with utmost precision to various sectors like Indian Defense, Automotive, Container Glass and other Mechanical Industry winning the hearts of his customers.
